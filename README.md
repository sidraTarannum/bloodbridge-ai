# 🩸 BloodBridge — Emergency Blood Network  

🚨 *1 missed call. 4 minutes. 1 life saved.*

BloodBridge is an AI-powered emergency blood coordination system that connects patients to nearby donors instantly — even without requiring a smartphone.

---

## 🚨 Problem  

Every year, thousands of lives are lost not due to lack of blood, but due to *poor coordination* between hospitals and donors.  

- Delay in finding donors  
- No real-time system  
- Lack of access in rural areas  

---

## 💡 Solution  

BloodBridge provides a *fast, intelligent, and accessible blood network*.

- 📞 Request via form / SMS / missed call  
- 🤖 AI-powered donor matching  
- 🔮 Blood shortage prediction  
- 📡 Offline emergency support  

---

## ⚙️ Features  

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Instant Donor Matching  
- Finds nearby donors instantly  
- Filters by blood group and availability  

### 🔮 AI Blood Shortage Prediction  
- Predicts shortages up to 7 days in advance  
- Uses demand trends and patterns  

### 📡 Offline SMS & Missed Call Support  
- Works without internet  
- Designed for rural and feature phone users  

### 💌 Donor Appreciation System  
- Sends personalized thank-you messages  
- Encourages repeat donations  

---

## 🤖 AI Usage  

BloodBridge uses *Gemini AI* for:

- Smart donor matching  
- Blood shortage prediction  
- Personalized thank-you messages  

> ⚠️ Note: Current prototype uses simulated AI logic, designed for real Gemini API integration.
